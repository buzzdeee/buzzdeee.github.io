## Hi, I'm Sebastian! 👋

I'm an IT and security enthusiast in my 50s. I focus on bridging the gap between high-security digital systems and the tangible craft of the hardware world.

### 🐡 OpenBSD & Open Source
As an **OpenBSD Ports & Package Maintainer**, I actively contribute to the ecosystem of one of the world's most secure operating systems. For me, security isn't just a job; it's a mindset.
* **Security & Network:** I port and maintain tools for both "Red" and "Blue" sides, along with specialized networking utilities.
* **Diversity:** My portfolio covers everything that was missing from the daily workflow—from software stacks like **GNUstep** to digital model railroad tools and even games.
* **Community:** I take pride in adopting orphaned ports and bringing new software to the platform to make OpenBSD even more versatile for everyone.

### 🛠️ Making & Hardware
When I'm not at the terminal, you'll likely find me in my workshop:
* **Hardware Tinkering:** PCB-level hacking and precision soldering of custom circuits.
* **3D Printing:** As a maker, I enjoy turning digital designs into physical objects.
* **Model Railroads:** Digitally controlling my layout perfectly blends my IT passion with classic craftsmanship.

### 🛹 Freestyle Skateboarding
A skateboard keeps you young. Freestyle skateboarding is my creative outlet—a sport that demands technical precision and relentless persistence, much like a well-crafted software port.

### 👨‍👩‍👦‍👦 Family
Outside of tech and sports, I’m a proud father of three boys. They make sure there’s never a dull moment at home and probably challenge me more often than any stubborn software port ever could.
